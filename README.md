Project Overview:
This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a subscription-based business model. The objective was to identify retention drivers, churn reasons, and provide strategic recommendations to reduce customer loss.

Business Objectives:
- Measure churn rate & retention trends
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Analyze lifecycle churn behavior
- Provide actionable retention strategies

Dashboard Preview
Key Findings:
- Early lifecycle customers are at highest churn risk
- Contract length significantly impacts retention
- Lack of technical support increases churn probability
- Electronic check users churn the most

Recommendations:
- Improve onboarding strategy
- Promote long-term contracts
- Encourage auto-pay enrollment
- Bundle support services

- Power BI

- Business Analytics
